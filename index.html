How To Import A Preset?

Tip
Presets include instructions and settings for models. Different ones offer different experiences. There is no "best" or "worst" preset; it's all a matter of your personal preference.
  1. Open SillyTavern.
  2. Go to the Chat Completion Presets tab by clicking the first icon from the left (sliders) at the top of the page.
  3. Click the Import preset button (the one with the little arrow pointing at paper) at the top of the screen.
  4. Select the file you want to import.
  5. Open it.

FAQ

Q: What preset should I use?
A: Use whatever works for you. If you get too many refusals with mine, try someone else's or write your own.
Q: Can I edit your preset?
A: Of course! I highly encourage it! You can use it as a base to build something personalized.
